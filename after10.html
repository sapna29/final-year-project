Career Assessment after 9th:

Yes, you do get to choose your subjects in Class 9.

There are three groups of subjects.

Group 1-Compulsory Subjects: English(Language and Literature) History, Civics and Geography Language(Language of your choice)

Group 2- Optional Subjects: For this group you need to choose 2 subjects. This group has you core subject. Most popular ones are Science, Maths, Commercial Studies, Economics etc.

Group 3- Optional Subject: For this group you need to choose an one subject. This will be an extra subject and will be considered as your sixth subject. Popular electives are Commercial Applications, Economic Applications, Home Science, Physical Education, Computer Applications, Physical Education etc. You need to choose subjects from what your school offers. Some schools don’t have a lot of options while others have many. Choose subjects which you have a genuine interest in. It really helps.

Which Stream to Choose After Class 10 And How?

Use the tips provided to determine Which Stream to Choose After Class 10 and is best for your future in India-

Know Your Interest- It is necessary to determine whether you are interested in a particular subject. All that is required is that the user select a stream to which he or she wishes to connect.

Find Your Strength- After reviewing the areas of interest, one must match their interests with their strengths. Choose the subjects you feel you can do well in.

Understand the Stream in Depth- After determining one’s strengths and areas of interest, one must consider the stream’s future growth after the tenth grade. So, after you’ve decided on one, make sure to look into its future potential.

Seek Career Guidance- Students can also get career advice from experts who are available both online and offline. These counsellors conduct regular research on various career options and student psychology in order to provide better guidance.

Financial Budget Check- Sometimes students have big dreams and the strength to pursue them, but their parents’ pockets won’t allow them to do so. In this case, students can choose a similar option for a lower fee.


STREAMS AFTER 10TH CLASS -

Science, Commerce, and Humanities/Arts are the three broad streams from which students can choose after class X. These three streams are classified broadly based on their course structure and subjects.

The following is information that every Class X student should read and consider before deciding which stream to pursue after 10th grade.

Science: This field is related to medical studies and engineering, and includes subjects such as physics, chemistry, and biology.

Commerce Stream: The Commerce Stream is concerned with trade, commerce, business, and financial marketing. Commerce is the best major for you if you have strong math and financial skills.

Humanities/Arts Stream: The broadest of all, this stream is concerned with education in subjects such as history, geography, political science, psychology, sociology, languages, anthropology, human resources, journalism, and so on.

Vocational Stream: This stream includes professional courses such as fashion design and nursing.
